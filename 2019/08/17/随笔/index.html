随笔 - 001

2019-08-17
#随笔

转眼入职已有一年有余，在一家提供检验服务的公司做程序员让我受益良多。
找工作的前几周，我住在一家废弃的工人宿舍，台风山竹来袭让闷热的天气稍缓了一些。

依稀记得去年8月8号，我入职时的情景。这一段时间一直都休息不好，生物钟六点准时
叫我起床，简单洗了头后，在楼下买了两个饼之后就坐上了地铁。上海早高峰的地铁总
是人多的，但我们在地铁的最终边，等来的第一班车总是能上去的。

刚工作的第一天，新白透一枚，对于企业级开发一无所知。之前在学校学到的东西仅仅
是一些模块化而浅显的知识。完全不懂得串联运用。面对一个个新的东西，好在我在做
这个工作的同时，有另一位出色的程序员帮助我，他总能简单一两句话就能说中一些什么。
因此在我职业生涯的第一步，他是一位非常重要的引路人。

我一向对自己的代码很有要求，但是写出来的东西直到目前为止仍然代码冗余。目前我会尽快克服这个问题。

以此我对自己仅提出三点要求：

  1. 保持学时
  2. 知识更新
  3. 认真睡眠

English Version

It has been more than a year since I joined the company. I benefited a lot from working as a programmer in a company providing inspection services.
For the first few weeks of my job search, I lived in an abandoned workers’ dormitory, and the sweltering heat was eased by the arrival of typhoon mangosteen.
I still vaguely remember the scene when I joined the company on August 8 last year.This time has been a bad rest, the clock at 6 o ‘clock on time
Wake me up, wash my hair briefly, buy two cakes downstairs and then take the subway.The subway station in Shanghai during the morning rush hour
It was crowded, but we were at the end of the subway.
My first day on the job, I was completely new and had no idea about enterprise development.What I learned in school is just that
It’s modular and superficial knowledge.I don’t know how to use tandem at all.Face each new thing, good thing I am doing
At the same time, I had another great programmer to help me, who could always say something in a sentence or two.
So in the first step of my career, he was a very important guide.
I’ve always been very demanding with my code, but what I’ve written so far is still redundant.At present, I will overcome this problem as soon as possible.
Therefore, I only put forward three requirements for myself:

  1. Maintain study hours
  2. Knowledge update
  3. Get some sleep
